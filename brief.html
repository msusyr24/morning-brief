Principle-Agent Problem

Alderman's framing: "You could put in 100 hours studying theory and make less EV than if you meditated for 10 minutes before every session" — and he believes the high-stakes community is 5 years from taking live tells seriously and 15 years from internalizing the psychological/spiritual dimension.

Prostate Cancer Screening — Peter Attia Podcast #388

Attia's episode focuses on the argument that current PSA screening guidelines are leading to late-stage diagnoses where survival outcomes are dramatically worse — his framing: "The timing of when you find the cancer is not a minor detail, it's arguably the determining factor in life and death." Diverse Organic Molecules Found on Mars

NASA's Curiosity rover used a novel chemical derivatization technique (TMAH thermochemolysis via the SAM instrument) to detect a broader suite of preserved organic molecules on Mars than previously identified — including aromatic compounds and fatty acids that survived for billions of years. This doesn't confirm life, but it significantly expands the known organic chemical inventory of Mars and demonstrates that complex organics can persist in Martian conditions, which has implications for both past habitability and future sample return missions.
